Our Facilities

Zirconia Crown

Over 40 years, zirconia has already been broadly used for equally commercial and industrial purposes. It is vastly biocompatible and remarkably robust in longevity. Applications for dentistry are found in zirconia crowns, bridges and dental implants. Here are some features of our Zirconia Crown  High Strength  Excellent Aesthetics view.  Smart transparent color blend with other teeth.  Retain more of the existing tooth.

Dentures

Dentures are prosthetic devices constructed to replace missing teeth, and are supported by the surrounding soft and hard tissues of the oral cavity. Conventional dentures are removable. However, there are many denture designs, some which rely on bonding or clasping onto teeth or dental implants.

PFM Crown

PFM is the traditional choice for simple to advanced combination cases. Using different metals such as base metal alloys(cobalt, nickel, chromium), titanium alloys, and gold alloys PFM restorations can be made. Here are some features of our PFM Crown and Bridge  Reliable metal-ceramic bond  Low hardness  Metal Framework designed by CAD/CAM.  High degree of intraoral comfort for patients due to high thermal stability  Biocompatible and corrosion-resistant

Implant Crown

A crown is a cap that looks like a tooth and is placed over an existing tooth. It can be used to repair a broken tooth. A dental implant is a surgical implant that replaces a missing tooth and has a crown placed on top to look and function like a normal tooth.

E-max Crown

Emax is an all-ceramic system that is based on Lithium disilicate glass, and consists of quartz, lithium dioxide, phosphoroxide, alumina, potassium oxide and trace elements. Emax based all-ceramic prostheses are rapidly gaining widespread popularity in cosmetic dentistry due to their excellent esthetics, durability, and strength.

Flexible Dentures

Flexible dentures are the recent improvement of traditional dentures to replace missing teeth with soft and transparent base. Flexible dentures are easy and comfortable to use and non-brittle, unlikely to shatter if dropped. They don’t have any metallic clips or attachments and adhesives are unneeded. Flexible dentures are non-allergic and most patients find chewing and speaking easier with it.
